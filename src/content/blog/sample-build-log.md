This is **demo content** used to preview the blog layout. It is not being presented as a completed project log.

Future posts will be concise engineering records rather than generic updates. Each post will cover:

- what the project was supposed to accomplish;
- the approach that was attempted;
- problems discovered during implementation;
- evidence from testing;
- what changed as a result; and
- the next improvement.

The goal is to show progress honestly and make each project easier to discuss in an interview.
